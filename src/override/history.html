Do Not Capture

If for some reason you don't want to capture a given site, e.g. Gmail, you may enter its address (e.g. mail.google.com) into the box below and press return or space to tag it. And if you don't want to capture a site that has the word "mail" as part of its address then you may enter the word "mail" by itself. This will match all sites containing "mail" in their address like mail.google.com (Gmail), mail.yahoo.com (Yahoo Mail) and mail.myCompany.com but it won't match sites like mail-123.xyz.com or www.MyMail.com. To match those, you may enter mail-123.xyz.com and mymail.com, respectively.

Another example:

You may not want to capture your Google sign-in page (which has no useful information) so in that case you may enter the full address accounts.google.com. Alternatively, you may enter "account" and "accounts" to match all sign-in pages that have the word account in their address (most do.)

Removing the tags will re-enable capturing.
